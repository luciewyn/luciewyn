Ensembâle Sozialpreis Region Basel_Identity Design, Basel CH

INFO

The Basel Region Social Prize Ensembâle recognizes initiatives, organizations, and individuals committed to fostering positive community life.
The prize, first awarded in 1999, honors projects that connect people, promote participation, and strengthen social cohesion.
Ensembâle is built on the idea of connection through flow, symbolizing collaboration and togetherness.
